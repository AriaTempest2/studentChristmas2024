Car Youtooz Figure

Who doesn't love a sequel? Back back back again is Car, this time in a Youtooz collectible figure form! Standing excitedly at 3.6 inches tall, his sharp angular body is on all fours as a short tail points up and back. With his body a gradient from a deep brown at the base and points to a taupe in the middle, Car is accessorized and ready to impress with a diamond glort in his left ear. The windowed display box has been designed with dark tones as shadowy silhouettes of trees and buildings spread across a dark sky. The exterior of their packaging sees a shadowy woods amongst a dark green sky. While the interior is solid black except for a white diamond. the protective sleeve continues the deep woods vibe as his figure can be seen through the large clear window at its front. This collectible was designed in collaboration with community member @therealracckun and ships in a matte, embossed, protective outer sleeve and a custom-sized plastic protector for maximum protection during shipping.
